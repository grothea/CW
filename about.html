A Local Coloradan with a passion for woodworking.

Hi, I'm Matt.

I was born and raised in Littleton, Colorado and love everything about my home state. In 2020, I remodeled a house and found my passion for woodworking.

I decided to combine my two passions and began Clark Woodworks from of my garage in Edgewater, CO. I make everything by hand and pride myself on making quality products that are practical and look great.
